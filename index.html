Welcome to Taylor's Trivia!

This is a game that tests your knowledge of how well you know Taylor.

The game will prompt you for different questions, and you must pick the best answer for each question. For each question you get right, you will get 10 points.

If you really know Taylor, you will score 100 points by the end of the game. If you don't know Taylor, it will be apparent. Maybe you can play again!
